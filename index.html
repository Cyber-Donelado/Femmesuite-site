About FemmeSuite

FemmeSuite is a marketplace and practice-management experience for beauty professionals and clients. Clients can request appointments, pay deposits, complete intake forms, and manage booking updates. Professionals can accept bookings, manage schedules, and process in-person or app-based payments through a secure payments stack.

This website includes the onboarding, payments, support, and legal pages typically requested for platform and payment reviews.
