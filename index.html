FAQ

Quick answers for clients and professionals.

How do payments work?

FemmeSuite supports deposits and final charges depending on booking policy. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.

How do professionals get paid?

Professional payouts are managed through onboarding and payout infrastructure powered by Stripe. See the Professional Onboarding page for details.

Do clients need to "check in"?

Check-in is designed to be low-friction. Booking and service lifecycle are tracked clearly, with optional timestamps to support dispute resolution.

How do I get support?

Email support@femmesuite.app or use the Contact Support page.
