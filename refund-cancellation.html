Refund & Cancellation

General platform guidance for appointment cancellations, reschedules, and refunds.

Client cancellations

Refund eligibility depends on the professional's cancellation policy, how close the cancellation is to the appointment time, and the booking/payment status. Some deposits may be non-refundable for late cancellations.

Professional changes

If a professional cannot make an appointment, FemmeSuite may support reschedule proposals and, in some flows, automatic deposit refunds. Clients are notified with clear next steps.

Payment reversals and disputes

FemmeSuite maintains booking and payment lifecycle records to support issue resolution. If you need help, contact support@femmesuite.app.
