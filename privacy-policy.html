Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

FemmeSuite ("we," "us," or "our") provides a platform for clients and beauty professionals to manage appointments and payments. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account info (name, email, phone)
  • Booking and communication data
  • Professional onboarding and payout information
  • Payment-related information processed via Stripe
  • Device/app usage information for reliability and security

How we use information

  • Provide and improve the FemmeSuite platform
  • Process bookings, payments, refunds, and payouts
  • Support merchant onboarding and compliance
  • Send transactional notifications (booking updates, reminders, receipts)
  • Prevent fraud and support dispute resolution

Payments

Payment processing and merchant services are powered by Stripe. Payment data is handled according to Stripe's security and compliance standards.
