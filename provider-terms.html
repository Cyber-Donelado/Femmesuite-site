Professional Provider Terms

Terms for beauty professionals using FemmeSuite to accept bookings and payments.

Provider responsibilities

  • Provide accurate service listings, pricing, and availability.
  • Honor accepted bookings or communicate changes promptly.
  • Maintain required licenses/certifications where applicable.
  • Follow local laws and health/safety requirements.

Payments and payouts

Professionals authorize FemmeSuite to facilitate payment processing and payouts through Stripe-powered infrastructure. Payout timing may depend on Stripe processing, account standing, and platform rules.

Compliance and verification

Professionals must provide accurate identity and business information during onboarding. Additional verification may be required to continue receiving payouts.

Support

Questions can be sent to support@femmesuite.app.
