# FemmeSuite - Mobile Beauty Service Marketplace

FemmeSuite is a premium beauty services marketplace platform that connects clients with trusted beauty professionals. The platform provides secure booking, appointment management, and integrated payment processing powered by Stripe.

## Features

- **Professional Marketplace**: Beauty professionals can list and manage their services
- **Booking Management**: Clients can request and manage beauty service appointments
- **Secure Payments**: Integrated payment processing through Stripe with deposit and final charge support
- **Merchant Onboarding**: Guided professional onboarding with identity verification
- **Client Portal**: Easy booking requests, payment methods, intake forms, and appointment management
- **Professional Dashboard**: Appointment scheduling, booking acceptance, and payout management
- **Tap to Pay on iPhone**: Support for contactless in-person payments using Stripe
